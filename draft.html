Project Overview & Objectives

Conducted a comprehensive business analysis of the Vietnamese aviation industry post-COVID-19, focusing on Vietnam Airlines and key competitors (Vietjet Air, Pacific Airlines, Bamboo Airways). The project aimed to forecast industry outlook, benchmark performance, analyze customer ratings, and develop actionable recommendations to enhance competitiveness and customer acquisition in the recovering market.

Key Findings & Insights

Industry Recovery

Vietnam's aviation sector is recovering rapidly from the pandemic, with passenger growth and market expansion expected to accelerate by 2035. Predictive models highlighted steady growth trajectories for passenger numbers, revenue, and operations.

Market Leadership

Vietnam Airlines ranked highest in customer preference and satisfaction, particularly for pricing, on-time performance, and service quality. 98% of respondents recognized it as their preferred future airline.

Customer Preference Factors

Primary factors influencing customer ratings included affordable prices, promotional offers, punctuality, and customer service. These insights provide clear direction for competitive positioning strategies.

Pricing Strategy Optimization

Recommended continuous monitoring and optimization of pricing strategies to maintain competitive advantage while maximizing revenue.

Customer Experience Enhancement

Advised Vietnam Airlines to maintain service excellence and invest in operations and airport experience improvements.

Digital Platform Investment

Recommended improvements to digital platforms and online customer touchpoints for enhanced user experience.

Suggested expanding partnerships and executing targeted marketing campaigns to increase competitiveness and customer loyalty.
